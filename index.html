Your Teen's Friendship Wiring

Every teen has a friendship wiring, a default way they show up with other people. None of the four is better than the others. Each one comes with a real strength and a specific friction.

This version is built for high school teens, grades 9 to 12 (roughly ages 14 to 18). If your child is younger, use our elementary and junior high version instead. This is about how your teen is wired socially, not a problem to fix. You are answering as the parent, from what you see. About 5 minutes, and you get a personalised read at the end.
